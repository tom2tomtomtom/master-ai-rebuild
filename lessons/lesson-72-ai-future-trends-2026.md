### 5 Pro Tips for 2026 Success

**Pro Tip 1: Start with Routine, Rule-Based Work**

Not all work can be automated equally.

Best candidates:
- Same workflow every time (no exceptions)
- Clear decision logic (if-then rules)
- High volume (worth automating)
- Time-consuming (saves real value)

Avoid:
- Creative work (agents aren't creative yet)
- Novel situations (agents need clear rules)
- Judgment calls (unless you can codify)
- Human relationships (not about efficiency)

**Pro Tip 2: Measure From Day 1**

Track:
- Time saved (hours/week)
- Cost saved ($ amount)
- Quality change (better/same/worse)
- Adoption rate (% of team using)
- ROI (savings / cost)

Share results monthly. Visible success unlocks resources.

**Pro Tip 3: Plan Workforce Transition NOW**

Don't surprise your team in 2026.

- Communicate: "Agents are coming, here's what changes"
- Show: New opportunities (better work)
- Train: Skills for new roles
- Create: Career paths in agent era

Handled well: Team excited
Handled poorly: Resistance, turnover

**Pro Tip 4: Build Internal Agent Capability**

Don't just buy tools. Build skill.

- Hire: At least 1 agent specialist
- Train: 3-5 people on agent building
- Experiment: Let them try new things
- Learn: Document what works/doesn't

Your competitive advantage: Knowledge, not just tools.

**Pro Tip 5: Assume 80/20 Rule for Specialization**

Specialized models will drive 2026 advantage.

- 80% of work: Generic agents (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- 20% of work: Specialized models (your competitive advantage)

Invest in the 20%. That's where you differentiate.
