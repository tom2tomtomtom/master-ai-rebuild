### Trend 1: Agentic Systems (AI That Does, Not Just Talks)

**What's Coming**:
- AI systems autonomously complete multi-step tasks
- Make decisions, take actions, validate results
- Handle complex business processes end-to-end
- Minimal human intervention needed

**Business Impact**:
- Categories of work eliminated (not just expedited)

3. Measure agent accuracy before automation
4. Plan workforce transition (not job loss, role change)

**Business Impact**:
- Better accuracy in specific domains

**Preparation**:

- Healthcare organizations (medical AI)
- Financial institutions (trading/risk AI)
- Manufacturing (quality control AI)
