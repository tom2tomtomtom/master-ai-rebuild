## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting & Pro Tips

**Problem 1: Resistance Stronger Than Expected**

*Symptom*:
- Complaints in Slack/hallways
- People skipping training
- "This will never work for us"
- Attrition uptick (people leaving)

*Root causes*:
- Insufficient addressing of job security fears
- Unclear business case
- Bad prior experience with change
- Wrong champion (not respected)

*Solution - Listen + Adjust:*

1. **Deep listening**
   ```
   Don't assume you know the concern
   Interview: 10-15 resisters
   Question: "What's your biggest worry about this?"

   Listen to answer, don't argue
   Acknowledge: "That's a real concern"
   Respond: With evidence, not promises
   ```

2. **Adjust messaging based on what you hear**
   ```
   If hearing: "I'll lose my job"
   â†’ More role evolution stories
   â†’ Connect with peer doing same job successfully
   â†’ Company commitment to no layoffs (if true)

   If hearing: "It's too complicated"
   â†’ Simpler training
   â†’ More 1:1 help
   â†’ "Look how easy this is" demos

   If hearing: "I don't trust it"
   â†’ Accuracy data
   â†’ What it can/can't do
   â†’ "You always review before using"
   ```

3. **Address with champions**
   - Are champions getting resistance?
   - What are they hearing?
   - Do they need support?
   - Are they the problem? (Wrong champions?)

**Problem 2: Early Adopters Frustrated with Laggards**

*Symptom*:
- Champions frustrated: "Why won't people just learn this?"
- Risk: Champions burnout
- Culture: "Us vs. them" forming

*Root causes*:
- Different change readiness (normal!)
- Champions don't understand change psychology
- Expectations too high too fast

*Solution - Educate + Normalize:*

1. **Train champions on change psychology**
   ```
   Rogers' Adoption Curve: Normal distribution

   Innovators (5%): Jump in day 1
   Early Adopters (15%): Join month 1-2
   Early Majority (35%): Join month 3-4
   Late Majority (35%): Join month 5-6
   Laggards (10%): Join month 7-12 (or not)

   "Getting to 80% adoption is normal
   Some people will take longer
   That's OK, it's expected"
   ```

2. **Give champions strategies for laggards**
   ```
   For resister:
   - Don't argue or persuade
   - Listen: "What would help you?"
   - Connect: Show peer success (not theory)
   - Offer: Specific help (no judgment)
   - Check-in: "Want to try this week?"
   - Accept: Maybe not this month (it's OK)
   ```

3. **Celebrate non-adopter turned adopter**
   ```
   When laggard finally adopts:
   - Celebrate publicly
   - Recognize champion who helped
   - "Welcome! Glad you're here"
   - Not: "About time"
   ```

**Problem 3: Adoption Stalls at 50% and Won't Move Further**

*Symptom*:
- Early majority onboarded
- But late majority not joining
- Feels stuck
- 6 weeks in, only 50% adoption

*Root causes*:
- Different strategies needed for late majority
- Social proof not yet strong
- Peer pressure not enough yet
- May need light mandate

*Solution - Change strategy for late majority:*

```
EARLY MAJORITY STRATEGY (Weeks 1-4):
Voluntary, education-focused
Risk: May hit ceiling at 40-50%

LATE MAJORITY STRATEGY (Weeks 5-8):
Social proof + peer pressure + gentle mandates
How:
1. "80% of your peers are using this"
2. "It's becoming standard in your function"
3. "Next feedback review mentions AI learning"
4. Champions: More peer pressure (normal for this group)
5. Simplify: Easier prompts, templates
6. Connect: Pair with power user
7. Timeline: Light mandate ("We're going to do this starting [date]")
```
