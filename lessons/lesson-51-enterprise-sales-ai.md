## ðŸŽ“ Progressive Mastery

### Level 1: Foundation (5 min) - Lead Scoring

**Scenario**: Sales team manually qualifies 50+ leads/month. Takes 1-2 hours/week.

**Mission**: Implement AI lead scoring to auto-qualify leads.

**Setup**:
1. Define scoring criteria (company size, industry, engagement)
2. Input historical data (won/lost deals)
3. Train AI model
4. Score all leads automatically

**Result**: Automatic lead scoring, top 20% of leads identified instantly

---

### Level 2: Intermediate (7 min) - Prospect Research & Outreach

**Scenario**: Sales rep spends 15 minutes researching each prospect before outreach.

**Mission**: AI research and generate personalized outreach.

**Workflow**:
1. AI researches prospect and company (real-time)
2. AI extracts key information (industry, recent news, challenges)
3. AI generates 3 personalized outreach subject lines
4. AI drafts personalized email
5. Sales rep selects best version and sends

**Result**: Personalized, researched outreach takes 3 minutes instead of 20

---

### Level 3: Advanced (8 min) - End-to-End Sales Automation

**Scenario**: Entire sales process (lead to close) has 50+ touchpoints.

**Mission**: AI assists in every touchpoint for faster, better sales.

**Workflow**:
- Lead scoring (auto-qualifies)
- Research (auto-gathers intel)
- Outreach (auto-generates emails)
- Meetings (auto-prepares for calls, takes notes)
- Proposals (auto-generates customized)
- Follow-ups (auto-schedules, auto-writes)
- Upsells (auto-identifies opportunities)
- Win/loss analysis (auto-analyzes deals)

**Result**: Sales process 50% faster, higher conversion
