## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting & Pro Tips

**Problem 1: AI-Generated Copy Lacks Brand Voice**
- Solution: Define voice in prompts
  ```
  "Use brand voice: Conversational, technical accuracy, empowering.
  Avoid: Corporate jargon, hype, buzzwords.
  Example: [paste 3 good emails]

  Generate email for [segment] about [topic]"
  ```
- Result: AI matches brand perfectly

**Problem 2: Segmentation Too Granular**
- Solution: Start with 3-5 segments, expand gradually
  - Segment 1: New leads (onboarding focus)
  - Segment 2: Active users (feature education)
  - Segment 3: At-risk (win-back offers)
  - Segment 4: High-value (expansion focus)
- Test each separately
- Don't over-complicate initially

**Problem 3: A/B Tests Inconclusive**
- Solution: Ensure statistical significance
  - Run minimum 100 users per variation
  - Run minimum 1 week per test
  - Use proper statistical calculators
  - Don't stop tests early
- Result: Clear winners emerge, confidence in changes

**Problem 4: Email Deliverability Issues**
- Solution: Follow best practices
  - Authenticated domain (DKIM, SPF, DMARC)
  - Proper unsubscribe link
  - Avoid spam trigger words
  - List hygiene (remove bounces)
- Most AI tools include compliance defaults

**Problem 5: ROI Not Improving**
- Solution: Debug the funnel
  - Measure: Clicks, conversions, revenue per campaign
  - AI can improve one metric but hurt another
  - Focus on: Qualified leads, not just volume
  - Verify: Leads are actually MQLs (not garbage)
