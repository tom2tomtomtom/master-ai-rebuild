## ðŸ”§ Troubleshooting & Pro Tips

### Common Problems & Solutions

**Problem 1: "Generated images look artificial/not realistic"**

Solution:
- Add photography references: "Shot like professional product photography"
- Specify quality: "High-end catalog photography, sharp focus, professional lighting"
- Remove artificial descriptors: avoid "AI generated," "digital art"
- Use specific photographer references: "In style of Annie Leibovitz" or "Like National Geographic photography"

**When to use**: All product/professional images
**Impact**: 70% improvement in realism and professionalism

---

**Problem 2: "Can't get consistent product colors"**

Solution:
```
For product [NAME], exact color specs:
- Primary color: [Pantone color code if available, or specific hex]
- Secondary: [Color code]
- Avoid: [Colors to not include]
```

Make color reference extremely specific (not "blue" but "royal blue #4169E1")

---

**Problem 3: "Text in images is wrong/unreadable"**

Solution:
- Use DALL-E 3 (best for text)
- Keep text simple (1-2 words)
- Don't rely on AI for textâ€”add text in Photoshop instead
- Some platforms struggle with textâ€”use when text-free
